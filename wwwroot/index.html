Who I am

I am an experienced software engineer and a whiz at SEO who constantly seeks out innovative solutions to everyday problems. My commitment to critical thinking, attention to detail and hard work has gotten me to where I am today.

I love writing code. Ever since writing my first program in Visual Basics for Applications and manipulating it to produce the desired output, I have been obsessed with the idea of using software to solve practical problems.

One of the most interesting projects I have accomplished recently was designing and developing an analytics chatbot (AI virtual assistance) that uses state-of-the-art natural language processing and machine learning techniques to provide actionable insights into oil and gas WRFM (Well, Resiervoir and Facility Managment) data. It improve data retrieval, perform analytics, and elevate the user experience. I found it so interesting because of the positive impact it has on the user workflow.

I integrated the chatbot in Microsoft Teams and its usage jumped by 67% within one month of launch. This contributed to a 93.75% increase in chatbot total chat logs.

I also enjoyed working with the rest of the machine learning engineers. The discussions we had about the pros and cons of each step were just exhilarating. I think it was such a joy to be able to see a project from start to finish. I learned a lot about teamwork, communication, and the importance of making and sticking to a plan.

Software engineering is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately enageged in solving. I believe in the power of programming to trnasform and improve the lives of people around the world.

In 2018 I started enterprise software developemt, and life has jut gotten better from there. I'm obseessed with application performance, user experincce, and simplicity.

Apart from writing software, I do have a few hobbies... I love listening to music, mostly hip-pop and rock (2Pac, Jay-Z, 50cent, Eminem, LilWayne, T.I, Snoop Dogg are among my faovrites artisits). I'm also a gamer. I love computer games. My favorites games are Actions, Adventure, Sport, and Mind games.
