BIO

C# .NET, WPF, MEAN, LAMP & SEO

Dedicated, passionate, and accomplished Full-stack .NET developer with over five years of hands-on professional experience creating RESTful APIs, web, mobile and desktop applications.

Intro

I possess a broad knowledge of the Microsoft technology stack, including C#, .NET Core, ASP.NET, .NET Framework, WPF, WinForm, and MS SQL Server.

I have experience developing REST APIs with Node.js and Single Page Application (SPA) with Angular.

I am familiar with SOLID design principles, object-oriented programming, test-driven development (TDD), domain-driven development (DDD), Model-ViewController (MVC), Model-View-ViewModel (MVVM), Machine Learning (ML), and CQRS.

I also excel at machine learning and natural language processing and am often involved with AI-related natural language processing projects. While working primarily in the tech industry, I have learned how to deliver critical software systems that are highly scalable, secure, and fast.

In addition to my technical background, I am an expert in search engine optimization (SEO) and can get your business ranked on top of the Google search engine result page (SERP).
